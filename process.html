Commission Process

  • You can contact me through Instagram, Discord, or Tumblr, just tell me you're interested in commissions straight on! (By asking, it means you agree to the TOS).
  • If I accept, I confirm your order and price (if you have anything you want to add or change, you should obviously tell me then!)
  • I send you the sketch for review.
  • If you confirm the sketch, I receive payment. I will confirm that the payment matches with what was agreed on and start the drawing.
  • I WILL NOT START THE DRAWING UNTIL YOU PAY!!
  • I will send screenshots of my progress (lineart, rendering, and bg [if applicable]). Minor changes such as addition of small accessories or color shifts can be made during then.
  • Once it's done, I'll send you the watermarked image (or images, if you didn't apply for complex bg. Then I send a transparent PNG with the regular JPEG) through email.
  • You're done!! Thanks in advance for your patience!
